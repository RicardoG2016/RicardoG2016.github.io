What mobile OS should you choose?




Currently Google's Android and Apple's IOS are the two most popular OS for mobile devices but how do you know which one is right for you? Below we will be comparing the two to help you decide. We will review each OS and list the pros, cons and provide our opinion on the OS.


Android

Current Version: Android Marshmallow 6.0

Pros

  • You have multiple devices and brands to choose from that run OS.
  • Highly customizable.
  • App store is less strict

Cons

  • Not all devices get OS updates.
  • Not as user friendly.
  • App store is significantly smaller than Apple's.

Opinion

For tech savvy individuals the Android may be a better choice because it's most customizable and provides more hardware options. However if you are looking for ease of use and stability then this might not be the choice for you.



IOS

Current Version: IOS 10.0

Pros

  • Most user friendly OS available.
  • Consistent across all devices.
  • Largest App store

Cons

  • Only runs on Apple devices.
  • Not as customizable.
  • Doesn't include all features available on Android.

Opinion

Apple's IOS provides stability, stale and predictability for those looking for an easy to use and reliable OS. However, these benefits come with a few drawbacks, mostly the ability to customize the OS and choose from different manufactures. But if you are willing to overlook those drawbacks then IOS is the OS for you.



Final Verdict

There is no right or wrong choice. Both these OS's are great and provide different benefits and drawbacks depending on the type fo user you are. For those looking for a more customizable OS Android will be favored while those looking for ease of use will lean towards IOS.
